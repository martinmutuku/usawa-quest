DISASTER MANAGEMENT

Don't wait for disaster to strike. Take action today to support our disaster management efforts and help communities prepare for and respond to crises. Visit our Disaster Management page to learn more about our programs and make a donation to support our work. Your contribution can make a real difference in the lives of those affected by disasters.

Livelihood:

At Usawa Quest Africa, we recognize the importance of livelihoods for sustainable development and poverty reduction. Our programs focus on building the capacity of communities to engage in income-generating activities, such as agriculture, small business development, and vocational training. By empowering individuals and communities to create their own livelihoods, we help to promote economic self-sufficiency and resilience.

Emergency Preparednes and Response:

Usawa Quest Africa is committed to ensuring that communities are prepared for emergencies and disasters. Our programs focus on building the capacity of communities to identify risks and develop preparedness plans, as well as providing emergency response training and support. By working with communities to prepare for emergencies, we can reduce the impact of disasters and save lives.

Emergency Health:

Usawa Quest Africa recognizes that access to healthcare is essential, especially in times of crisis. Our emergency health programs focus on providing life-saving medical care and supplies, such as emergency surgery, trauma care, and maternal and child health services. By ensuring that healthcare services are available and accessible during emergencies, we can help to prevent needless suffering and loss of life

Water and Sanitization Hardware:

At Usawa Quest Africa, we believe that access to clean water and sanitation is a basic human right. Our programs focus on providing safe water and sanitation facilities to communities, as well as promoting hygiene education and behavior change. By providing access to clean water and sanitation, we can help to prevent waterborne diseases and improve the overall health and well-being of communities.

Disaster Risk Reduction and Climate Chanfe Adaptation (DRR /CCA):

Usawa Quest Africa recognizes the need to address the impacts of climate change and the increasing frequency and severity of disasters. Our programs focus on building the capacity of communities to identify and mitigate risks, as well as adapting to the impacts of climate change. By working with communities to reduce disaster risks and build resilience, we can help to prevent loss of life and livelihoods.
