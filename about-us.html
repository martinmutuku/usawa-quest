Our Vision

Our vision at Usawa Quest Africa is to create a world where communities are empowered to build sustainable, just, and peaceful societies. We envision a world where everyone has access to basic needs such as food, water, health care, education, and livelihoods. We strive to achieve this vision through our programs, partnerships, and advocacy efforts.

Our Mission

At Usawa Quest Africa, our mission is to empower communities to achieve their full potential. We work to promote sustainable development, social justice, and human rights through community-driven programs that build resilience and self-sufficiency. Our mission is driven by the belief that every individual has the right to live a dignified and fulfilling life.

Our History

Usawa Quest Africa was founded in 2020 by a group of individuals who wanted to make a difference in the lives of communities facing poverty, inequality, and injustice. Since then, we have grown to become a leading organization in community-driven development and humanitarian response in Africa. Our programs have reached thousands of individuals and communities, providing them with the tools and resources they need to build sustainable and resilient livelihoods.

Our Core Values

At Usawa Quest Africa, our core values guide our work and shape our organizational culture. We believe in the value of:

Empowerment

We believe that communities should be empowered to take charge of their own development.

Partnership

We believe in the importance of collaboration and partnership with local communities, civil society organizations, and government agencies.

Equity

We believe in the principle of fairness and strive to address inequalities in all aspects of our work.

Transparency

We are committed to transparency and accountability in our work.

Innovation

We are open to new ideas and innovative approaches to development challenges.
