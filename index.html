Featured Projects

Community Epidemic and Pandemic Preparedness

Usawa Quest Africa has been working with communities to prepare for and respond to epidemics and pandemics. Our programs include community education and awareness-raising on prevention measures, as well as the development of community-based response plans. During the COVID-19 pandemic, we have been supporting local health systems and providing communities with essential supplies such as PPE and hygiene kits.

GBV Awareness

Usawa Quest Africa is committed to ending gender-based violence (GBV) and promoting gender equality. Our GBV awareness programs aim to raise awareness about the harmful effects of GBV and provide support for survivors. We work with communities, schools, and other organizations to promote gender equality and to empower women and girls to become agents of change in their communities.

Read More

Tracing and Refugee Response

Usawa Quest Africa provides assistance to refugees and internally displaced persons (IDPs) who have been forced to flee their homes due to conflict or other crises. Our programs include tracing and reunification services for families who have been separated, as well as support for the basic needs of refugees and IDPs such as food, water, shelter, and healthcare. We also work with host communities to promote social cohesion and peaceful coexistence.

Read More

Livelihoods and Economic Empowerment

Usawa Quest Africa recognizes the importance of sustainable livelihoods for building resilience and reducing poverty. Our livelihoods and economic empowerment programs aim to support communities to develop income-generating activities and access financial services. We work with communities to identify their needs and develop sustainable solutions that are appropriate to their context. Our programs include training and capacity building in areas such as agriculture, entrepreneurship, and financial management.

More Projects

  1. Empowering Women and Girls through Education At Usawa Quest Africa, we believe that education is key to empowering women and girls to reach their full potential. That's why we work to provide access to education for girls in rural communities, supporting them in their academic and personal growth. Our programs aim to break down barriers to education, including poverty, cultural norms, and gender discrimination. By empowering women and girls through education, we can create a brighter future for individuals, families, and communities.
  2. Responding to Emergencies and Disasters Usawa Quest Africa is committed to providing rapid response and support to communities affected by emergencies and disasters. Our disaster management programs aim to provide life-saving assistance to those in need, including food, water, shelter, and medical care. We work closely with local partners and communities to ensure that resources are distributed effectively and efficiently, helping to support long-term recovery efforts.
  3. Building Sustainable Communities Sustainability is at the heart of our work at Usawa Quest Africa. We believe that building sustainable communities is essential for creating a brighter future for all. That's why our programs focus on supporting sustainable agriculture, renewable energy, and environmental conservation. By working with communities to build sustainable practices and systems, we can create a better world for generations to come.
  4. Supporting Youth Leadership and Development At Usawa Quest Africa, we believe that young people are the future leaders of our world. That's why our programs aim to support youth leadership and development, empowering young people to become agents of change in their communities. Our youth programs focus on providing mentorship, leadership training, and skills development, helping to prepare young people for the challenges and opportunities of the future.
  5. Advocating for Human Rights and Social Justice Usawa Quest Africa is committed to advocating for human rights and social justice in all aspects of our work. We believe that everyone deserves to live a life free from violence, discrimination, and injustice. That's why our programs focus on promoting human rights, equality, and social justice, working to break down barriers and create a more just and equitable world for all.
  6. Supporting Health and Wellness in Communities At Usawa Quest Africa, we believe that good health is essential for individuals, families, and communities to thrive. That's why our programs focus on promoting health and wellness, providing access to healthcare services and education on healthy living. We work with communities to identify health needs and develop sustainable solutions, supporting long-term health outcomes and improving overall well-being.
  7. Strengthening Peace and Conflict Resolution Usawa Quest Africa is committed to promoting peace and conflict resolution in communities affected by conflict and violence. Our programs aim to build peace from the ground up, supporting local peacebuilding efforts and working to prevent conflict and violence before it begins. By bringing communities together to address the root causes of conflict and promote understanding and cooperation, we can create a more peaceful and just world.
  8. Fostering Entrepreneurship and Economic Development At Usawa Quest Africa, we believe that entrepreneurship and economic development are essential for creating sustainable communities and empowering individuals to reach their full potential. That's why our programs focus on supporting small businesses, providing training, mentorship, and access to finance. By fostering entrepreneurship and economic development, we can help to create jobs, reduce poverty, and build strong, resilient communities.
  9. Supporting Refugees and Internally Displaced Persons Usawa Quest Africa is committed to supporting refugees and internally displaced persons affected by conflict and displacement. Our programs aim to provide life-saving assistance, including food, shelter, water, and medical care, to those who have been forced to flee their homes. We work closely with local partners and communities to provide support and ensure that refugees and internally displaced persons are able to rebuild their lives in safety and dignity.
