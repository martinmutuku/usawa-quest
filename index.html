Featured Projects

Community Epidemic and Pandemic Preparedness

Usawa Quest Africa has been working with communities to prepare for and respond to epidemics and pandemics. Our programs include community education and awareness-raising on prevention measures, as well as the development of community-based response plans. During the COVID-19 pandemic, we have been supporting local health systems and providing communities with essential supplies such as PPE and hygiene kits.

GBV Awareness

Usawa Quest Africa is committed to ending gender-based violence (GBV) and promoting gender equality. Our GBV awareness programs aim to raise awareness about the harmful effects of GBV and provide support for survivors. We work with communities, schools, and other organizations to promote gender equality and to empower women and girls to become agents of change in their communities.

Read More

Tracing and Refugee Response

Usawa Quest Africa provides assistance to refugees and internally displaced persons (IDPs) who have been forced to flee their homes due to conflict or other crises. Our programs include tracing and reunification services for families who have been separated, as well as support for the basic needs of refugees and IDPs such as food, water, shelter, and healthcare. We also work with host communities to promote social cohesion and peaceful coexistence.

Read More

Livelihoods and Economic Empowerment

Usawa Quest Africa recognizes the importance of sustainable livelihoods for building resilience and reducing poverty. Our livelihoods and economic empowerment programs aim to support communities to develop income-generating activities and access financial services. We work with communities to identify their needs and develop sustainable solutions that are appropriate to their context. Our programs include training and capacity building in areas such as agriculture, entrepreneurship, and financial management.
